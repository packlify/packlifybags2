Why Flat Bottom Pouches Work

The flat bottom pouch (block-bottom box pouch) is the premium format choice for brands that demand maximum shelf presence. With five printable panels—front, back, two side gussets, and a flat rectangular base—it provides 40% more printable surface than a standard stand up pouch. The flat bottom design creates a rigid, self-standing block profile that merchandises beautifully on shelf. Coffee roasters, premium pet food brands, and high-end snack companies consistently choose this format to elevate their retail positioning.

  • Flat rectangular base provides rock-solid shelf stability without leaning
  • Five printable panels for 360-degree branding and ingredient storytelling
  • Premium finish options: soft-touch matte, registered matte/gloss, spot UV
  • Square or rectangular footprint optimizes shelf and case packing density
  • Compatible with automated filling: side-gusset feeding on rotary fillers

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes flat bottom pouches different from stand up pouches?

Flat bottom pouches have a rectangular base and five distinct panels (front, back, two sides, bottom), providing 40% more printable surface and a rigid block profile. Stand up pouches have a rounded bottom gusset with three panels.

Are flat bottom pouches compatible with my filling equipment?

Yes. They are typically filled on rotary machines with side-gusset feeding attachments. Our technical team provides a filling-line compatibility sheet for your specific equipment before production.

Can I have a clear window on a flat bottom pouch?

Yes. Round, oval or full-side transparent windows can be integrated into the front or side panel. Window placement is confirmed during the pre-press proof stage.

What finish options are best for premium coffee packaging?

Soft-touch matte with registered spot gloss on the logo and key callouts is the most popular combination for specialty coffee. Cold foil stamping adds a premium metallic accent.
