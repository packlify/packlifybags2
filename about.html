About Packlify Bags — Your Global Flexible Packaging Partner

From our manufacturing base in China, we serve brands in 50+ countries with custom pouches, bags, and rollstock film built to exact barrier, print and filling-line specifications.

Company Overview

Packlify Bags was founded to bridge the gap between Western packaging buyers and high-quality Asian flexible packaging manufacturing. With 8+ years of hands-on production experience, we have shipped over 500 brand projects across coffee, pet food, snacks, beverages, personal care, and household product categories.

Our facility operates under ISO 9001 quality management and BRC packaging standards, with dedicated in-house teams for pre-press artwork, cylinder engraving, lamination, slitting, pouch making and final QC inspection. We are not a trading intermediary—we control the full manufacturing chain from raw film procurement to finished pouch delivery, which allows us to offer competitive pricing, consistent quality, and direct accountability on every order.

We specialize in custom flexible packaging, not off-the-shelf stock items. Every project starts with a needs assessment: product type, target shelf life, filling equipment, retail environment, and brand positioning. From there, we recommend material structures, print treatments, closure types and size configurations that match the technical and marketing requirements.

Core Capabilities

Material Engineering

Multi-layer laminates including PET/AL/PE, PET/VMPET/PE, NY/PE, Kraft/AL/PE, and high-barrier co-extrusions. OTR and WVTR testing for every new structure to validate barrier claims before production.

Printing & Finishing

Rotogravure up to 10 colors with Pantone matching. Digital HP Indigo for short runs. Available finishes: matte, gloss, soft-touch, registered matte, spot UV, holographic, and cold foil stamping.

Quality Control

Inline and offline QC at every stage: incoming film inspection, lamination bond strength testing, seal integrity checks, visual defect sorting, and final AQL sampling per ISO 2859-1. Third-party lab testing available upon request.

Logistics & Compliance

FOB, CIF, and DDP shipping terms. Standard export documentation including certificate of origin, fumigation certificate, and FDA food-contact compliance letter. BRC packaging certification for food-grade projects.

Production Process

1. Project Brief

Share your product type, target dimensions, quantity, barrier needs, and artwork concept. We return a material and pricing recommendation within 24 hours.

2. Artwork & Pre-Press

Our pre-press team reviews your artwork files for print feasibility, color separation, and bleed. We provide a digital proof for approval before cylinder engraving.

3. Sampling

Digital samples ship in 5-7 days; rotogravure cylinder samples in 10-15 days. Samples represent the full material structure, print quality and closures you will receive in production.

4. Production

Approved samples enter production queue. Standard lead time is 15-25 days. Inline QC monitors seal integrity, print registration, and dimensional accuracy throughout the run.

5. Final QC & Shipment

AQL sampling inspection before packing. Cartons labeled and palletized for export. Sea or air freight arranged with full tracking and customs clearance support.

Quality & Certifications

We maintain rigorous quality management systems to ensure every shipment meets the standards required for international food-contact packaging:

  • ISO 9001:2015 — Quality management system certification covering all production, inspection and customer service processes.
  • BRC Packaging Certified — Global Standard for Packaging and Packaging Materials, required by many major retailers and food brands.
  • FDA Compliance — Food-contact materials certified compliant with FDA 21 CFR for indirect food additives.
  • EU 10/2011 — Overall migration testing for plastic materials and articles intended to come into contact with food.
  • REACH / ROHS — Compliance with EU regulations on chemical substances and hazardous materials in packaging.

Third-party lab testing reports (SGS, Intertek, TUV) for barrier properties, heavy metals, and overall migration are available upon request for qualified projects.
