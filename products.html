Custom Flexible Packaging Products — 9 Formats for Every Brand

From retail-ready stand up pouches to high-speed pillow bag rollstock, explore nine custom packaging formats engineered for shelf impact, barrier protection, and filling-line compatibility.

Stand Up Pouches

Doypack with bottom gusset. Most versatile format for coffee, snacks, powders, and pet treats. Zipper, valve, and window options.

View Details

Flat Pouches

Three-side seal sachet format for single-serve portions, samples, and lightweight dry products. Tear notch and hang hole options.

View Details

Flat Bottom Pouches

Block-bottom box pouch with five display panels. Premium format for coffee beans and high-end pet food with maximum billboard space.

View Details

Side Gusset Bags

Expandable side panels for bulk fills up to 40 lb. Ideal for whole bean coffee, tea, powders, and large-format dry goods.

View Details

Quad Seal Pouches

Four-corner sealed box pouch with rigid shelf stance. Premium chips, granola, and snack brands choose this for maximum shelf presence.

View Details

Spout Pouches

Liquid-ready pouches with pour fitments for juices, sauces, gels, and cleaning products. Leak-proof seal, top or corner spout positions.

View Details

Die Cut Bags

Custom contour-cut mylar bags for distinctive shelf silhouettes. Seasonal launches, limited editions, and premium merchandising.

View Details

Pillow Bags

Back-seal rollstock format for high-speed VFFS lines. Snacks, confectionery, dry foods. Full-surface rotogravure print.

View Details

Packaging Film

Custom printed rollstock in barrier laminates for automatic packing lines. PET/PE, BOPP, foil laminates available.

View Details
