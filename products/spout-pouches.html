Why Spout Pouches Work

Spout pouches combine the material efficiency and branding surface of flexible packaging with the pouring convenience of a rigid fitment. For liquid and semi-liquid products—juices, sauces, gels, and cleaning products—spout pouches deliver a premium consumer experience at 55% less packaging weight than equivalent PET bottles.

  • Integrated PP spout fitment for controlled, drip-free pouring
  • 55%+ weight reduction vs. rigid bottles; lower shipping costs and carbon footprint
  • Ultra-high barrier laminates (PET/AL/NY/PE) for liquid product protection
  • Tamper-evident cap for consumer safety and retail compliance
  • Top or corner spout position based on category and dispensing ergonomics

Frequently Asked Questions

Are spout pouches leak-proof during shipping?

Yes. We perform drop testing at 1.5 m, burst testing at 400 kPa, and 30-day accelerated transport simulation on all spout pouch orders before shipment. The PET/AL/NY/PE structure and heat-sealed fitment integration are designed for leak-proof integrity.

Can I choose the spout position?

Yes. Top-center spout is standard for beverages and most liquid categories. Corner spout is available for products where angled pouring is preferred, such as cooking oils and sauces.

Are spout pouches suitable for hot-fill products?

Yes. We offer heat-resistant laminate structures and fitments rated for hot-fill temperatures up to 85-90 degree C. Please specify hot-fill requirements in your project brief.

What cap options are available?

Standard tamper-evident screw cap, flip-top sports cap, child-resistant cap, and custom-color caps matched to your brand palette.
