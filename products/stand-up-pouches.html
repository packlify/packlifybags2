Why Stand Up Pouches Work

The stand up pouch (doypack) is the most versatile flexible packaging format on the market. With a stable bottom gusset that expands on fill, it delivers excellent shelf presence at a lower material and shipping weight than rigid containers. For brands in coffee, snacks, pet food, supplements, and dry foods, the stand up pouch offers the ideal balance of barrier protection, print impact, and cost efficiency.

  • Self-standing bottom gusset for stable shelf display in any retail environment
  • Full-surface rotogravure print across front, back and gusset panels
  • High-barrier laminates (PET/AL/PE, PET/VMPET/PE, Kraft/AL/PE) protect aroma and freshness
  • Optional press-to-close zipper, one-way degassing valve, tear notch, hang hole and clear window
  • Compatible with automated filling lines (rotary and VFFS with doypack attachment)

Optional Features & Closures

  • Press-to-close zipper (single or double track)
  • Tear notch or laser score for easy open
  • One-way degassing valve (coffee)
  • Clear window (round, oval, or full-side transparent)

  • Child-resistant zipper configuration

Printing Options

Rotogravure up to 10 colors with Pantone matching for production runs. Digital HP Indigo for short runs and sampling. Full bleed coverage on all printable panels. Available finishes: matte, gloss, soft-touch, registered matte/gloss, spot UV, and cold foil.

Minimum Order Quantity & Sampling

Flexible MOQ from approximately 5,000 units for digital print and 10,000-20,000 units for rotogravure. Exact MOQ depends on laminate structure, dimensions, and feature complexity. Digital samples ship in 5-7 days; rotogravure cylinder samples in 10-15 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for custom stand up pouches?

Flexible MOQ starts around 5,000 units for digital print and 10,000-20,000 for rotogravure. We evaluate each project individually and can accommodate smaller trial runs.

Can I add a degassing valve to my stand up pouch?

Yes. We integrate one-way degassing valves for coffee and products that release CO2 after packaging. Valves are positioned based on your artwork layout and filling-line requirements.

What zipper options are available for stand up pouches?

Single-track and double-track press-to-close zippers, child-resistant zippers, and hook-to-hook closure strips. Zipper placement is confirmed during the pre-press stage.

Are custom stand up pouches compatible with automatic filling machines?

Yes. We configure pouch dimensions, film stiffness, slip properties, and zipper placement to match your specific filling equipment—rotary fillers with doypack attachments and VFFS machines with pouch-making capability.
