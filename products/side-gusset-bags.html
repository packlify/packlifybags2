Why Side Gusset Bags Work

Side gusset bags offer expandable side panels that accommodate high-volume fills while maintaining a clean, squared profile on shelf. For products like whole bean coffee in 2-5 lb formats, bulk tea, protein powders, and large-format pet treats, the side gusset design provides the cubic capacity of a box without the weight and cost of rigid packaging.

  • Expandable side gusset panels for high-volume fills from 1 lb to 40 lb
  • Squared profile stands stably on shelf without additional display support
  • High-barrier laminates protect product freshness for 12+ months
  • Optional press-to-close zipper, tin tie, valve, and hang hole configurations
  • Efficient case packing: packs flat before filling, expands to full volume on fill

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the maximum fill weight for side gusset bags?

Depending on product density and bag dimensions, side gusset bags can accommodate fills from 1 lb up to approximately 40 lb. We design bag dimensions around your specific fill weight and product density.

Are side gusset bags compatible with automatic filling?

Yes. They are typically filled on rotary or volumetric fillers with wide-mouth opening capability. Our technical team provides filling-line compatibility guidance for your specific equipment.

What is the difference between side gusset bags and stand up pouches?

Side gusset bags have expandable accordion-style side panels that increase cubic capacity, making them ideal for bulk fills (2-40 lb). Stand up pouches have a single bottom gusset and are better suited for retail-sized fills (4-16 oz).
