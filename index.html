Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ starts at 5,000 pieces for digitally printed pouches and 10,000 for rotogravure printed packaging. The exact MOQ depends on pouch size, material structure, and printing method. We work with startups and established brands alike.

How long does sampling and production take?

Digital print samples ship in 5-7 business days. Rotogravure samples with cylinder preparation take 10-15 days. Bulk production typically ships within 15-25 days after sample approval, depending on order volume and complexity.

What materials do you use for food-grade packaging?

We use multi-layer laminates such as PET/AL/PE for high barrier, PET/VMPET/PE for metallized finish, and Kraft/AL/PE for paper-touch exteriors. All food-contact layers comply with FDA and EU 10/2011 regulations.

Do you offer custom printing and finishes?

Yes. We offer rotogravure printing up to 10 colors and digital printing for short runs. Available finishes include matte, gloss, soft-touch, registered matte, spot UV, holographic, and clear window options.

Can you ship to my country?

We ship globally via sea freight (FCL/LCL) and air express. Common destinations include the US, Canada, UK, EU, Australia, Middle East, and Southeast Asia. We handle export documentation including certificate of origin and fumigation certificates.
