Why Flat Pouches Work

Flat pouches (three-side seal) are the simplest and most cost-effective flexible packaging format. With minimal material usage and straightforward production, they are the go-to choice for single-serve sachets, product samples, tea bag overwraps, and lightweight dry products where shelf stand-up is not required. Their compact footprint also makes them ideal for e-commerce insertion and subscription box sampling programs.

  • Three-side seal construction for maximum material efficiency and compact footprint
  • Full-surface print across both front and back panels
  • Available in clear, metallized or foil barrier structures
  • Optional tear notch for easy consumer opening
  • Optional hang hole (round or euro-slot) for pegboard retail display

Printing Options

Rotogravure up to 10 colors with Pantone matching. Digital HP Indigo for short runs. Both front and back panels are fully printable. We recommend matte finish for a premium sample feel and gloss for vibrant product photography.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for flat pouches?

Flexible MOQ starts around 5,000 units for digital print and 15,000-30,000 for rotogravure, depending on pouch dimensions and material structure.

Are flat pouches suitable for liquid products?

Standard flat pouches are designed for dry and semi-dry products. For liquids, we recommend spout pouches or flat pouches with specialized sealant layers—please discuss your specific product with our team.

Can flat pouches be resealable?

Flat pouches do not typically include zippers due to their narrow seal area. For resealable functionality, consider stand up pouches with a press-to-close zipper.
