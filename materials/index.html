Layer Functions Explained

Outer Layer (Print)

PET, BOPP, NY, Kraft Paper
Provides the print surface, stiffness, and exterior appearance. PET offers excellent print clarity; NY adds puncture resistance; Kraft gives a natural paper touch.

Barrier Layer

Aluminum Foil (AL), Metallized PET (VMPET), EVOH
Controls oxygen and moisture transmission. AL foil provides the highest barrier (<0.1 OTR). VMPET offers good barrier with lighter weight. EVOH is used in clear high-barrier structures.

Inner Sealant Layer

PE (LLDPE, LDPE), CPP
Provides heat-seal integrity and product contact surface. LLDPE offers excellent hot-tack and seal-through-contamination properties for high-speed filling lines.

Tie / Adhesive Layers

Solventless or solvent-based PU adhesive
Bonds dissimilar layers into a single film structure. The adhesive system is selected based on the product type, filling temperature, and required chemical resistance.

How to Choose the Right Structure

Material selection is the single most important decision in a custom packaging project. We recommend considering these factors in order:

  1. Product Sensitivity: Does your product require oxygen barrier (to prevent oxidation/rancidity), moisture barrier (to prevent clumping/staling), light barrier (for UV-sensitive ingredients), or aroma barrier (to retain volatile flavors or odors)?
  2. Target Shelf Life: How many months must the product remain fresh on the shelf at ambient or refrigerated conditions? This drives the required OTR and WVTR specifications.
  3. Filling Method: Will the pouch run on a VFFS machine, HFFS machine, rotary filler, or be hand-filled? Different filling methods require different film stiffness, slip properties, and heat-seal characteristics.
  4. Retail Environment: Will the pouch hang on a peg, stand on a shelf, or be displayed in a merchandising tray? This affects the required stiffness, bottom gusset design, and hang-hole specification.
  5. Brand & Finish: Matte, gloss, soft-touch, holographic, or Kraft-touch—the surface finish communicates your brand's quality tier and category positioning.
