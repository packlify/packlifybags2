Our Custom Packaging Development Process

1. Discovery & Needs Assessment

We analyze your product type, target shelf life, filling equipment, retail environment, and brand positioning. This assessment drives every downstream decision on materials, structure, and print treatment.

2. Material Recommendation

Based on barrier requirements (OTR, WVTR), product compatibility, and budget, we recommend one or more laminate structures. Options include PET/AL/PE, PET/VMPET/PE, Kraft/AL/PE, NY/PE, and custom co-extrusions.

3. Artwork & Pre-Press

Our pre-press team reviews your design files for print feasibility, color separation, and cylinder layout. We provide a digital or physical proof for your approval before engraving rotogravure cylinders.

4. Sampling

Digital print samples ship in 5-7 business days. Rotogravure samples with cylinder preparation ship in 10-15 days. Every sample represents the full material structure, print quality, and closures you will receive in production.

5. Production & QC

Approved samples enter the production queue. Standard lead time is 15-25 days. Inline and offline QC monitors seal integrity, print registration, dimensional accuracy, and visual defects throughout the run.

6. Final Inspection & Global Shipping

AQL sampling per ISO 2859-1 before packing. Cartons labeled and palletized for export. Sea freight (FCL/LCL) or air express with full customs documentation support.

Customization Options

Material Structures

PET/AL/PE for maximum barrier, PET/VMPET/PE for metallized finish, Kraft/AL/PE for natural paper touch, NY/PE for puncture resistance and clarity.

Printing Methods

Rotogravure up to 10 colors for large runs with exceptional color consistency. Digital HP Indigo for short runs and variable data. Combination print available.

Closures & Features

Press-to-close zippers, tear notches, laser scoring, hang holes, degassing valves, clear windows (round, oval, or full-side), spout fitments (top or corner), and child-resistant closures.

Surface Finishes

Matte, gloss, soft-touch, registered matte/gloss, spot UV, holographic, cold foil stamping, and embossed textures. Custom finish combinations available.
