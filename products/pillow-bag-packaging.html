Why Pillow Bags Work

Pillow bags (back-seal format) are the workhorse of high-speed snack and confectionery packaging. Produced in rollstock form and formed/filled/sealed on VFFS machines at speeds of 60-120 packs per minute, they offer the lowest per-unit cost for high-volume FMCG lines. Full-surface rotogravure print delivers vibrant brand graphics across the entire pack surface.

  • Back-seal format for high-speed VFFS lines (60-120 ppm)
  • Rollstock delivery for seamless integration with automated filling equipment
  • Full-surface rotogravure print for maximum brand visibility
  • Barrier laminates for moisture and oxygen-sensitive products
  • Lowest per-unit packaging cost for high-volume product lines

Printing Options

Rotogravure up to 10 colors with Pantone matching. Full-surface print on the entire rollstock web. Registration marks printed at regular intervals for VFFS machine eye-mark detection. Cold seal adhesive pattern can be applied for heat-sensitive products like chocolate.

Common Applications

This format is commonly selected for:

  • confectionery and candy bars

  • cookies and biscuits

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order for pillow bag rollstock?

Minimum orders start around 20,000 linear meters of printed rollstock. The number of finished packs depends on your bag dimensions. Contact us with your target bag size for a pack-count estimate.

Are pillow bags compatible with my VFFS machine?

Yes. We configure film stiffness, slip properties, sealant type (hot-seal or cold-seal), and eye-mark registration to match your specific VFFS machine model and operating speed.

What products should not use pillow bags?

Pillow bags are not recommended for liquid products or products requiring a resealable closure. For those applications, consider spout pouches or stand up pouches with zipper.
